Piqora's Data Policy

Your Google Analytics, Omniture, and Core Metrics data is kept safe and secure in Piqora's secure databases. The data is solely for the use of the signed-in user and is not accessible by 3rd parties, competitors, or any other customer of Piqora.

Google Analytics, Omniture, and Core Metrics helps brands track their revenue and clicks originating from their Pinterest pins. Piqora provides campaign recommendations to keep these core metrics growing.
