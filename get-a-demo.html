Frequently asked questions

  • How does Piqora help me Drive ROI on Pinterest?

    An annual subscription to Piqora’s marketing suite includes access to the only visual web analytics that enable you to track your top images, revenue from Pinterest, and your competitors’ content performance. Piqora shows you not only the revenue that’s directly attributable to Pinterest, but also the images that drove the referral traffic that lead to the orders. Use the insights from your top images to generate content that leads to more revenue, and the cycle continues!

  • How does Piqora help me get more followers on Instagram?

    The best way to grow your community on Instagram is to launch a photo contest. Piqora has powered 500+ successful promotions that have generated an average of 2.1 million impressions and thousands of new followers. Every Instagram promotion with Piqora benefits from the experience of a dedicated Customer Success Manager that will help you create a strategy and execute perfectly.

  • Do Hashtags Matter on Pinterest?

    Not really. Since about 90% of your brand’s pins on Pinterest come from your website, they will naturally have your product description and keywords in the pin (if your site has been pin-optimized).

    Discovery on Pinterest is based on following individual boards and search.
